Why You Should Consider Affordable Housing Preservation

In addition to strengthening your commitment to help more families with very low-, low- and moderate-income attain and sustain homeownership, here are other reasons to consider shared equity programs.


  • May help you meet your Community Reinvestment Act (CRA) mandates – based on your CRA strategy.
  • Increase your affordable footprint – provides entry into a niche market and help you strengthen your affordability goals
  • Expand business opportunities – These programs are not markets that are served. If borrowers are trying to capture a new market within affordable space, this could be something that they want to consider.

Freddie Mac will buy mortgages secured by properties subject to income-based resale restrictions and properties in community land trusts so you will have access to liquidity in the secondary mortgage market. Freddie Mac believes that shared equity providers are entities that may help mitigate losses if a borrower is in default.  Studies show that shared equity homeowners were 10 times less likely to be in foreclosure than homeowners in the conventional market across all incomes.
