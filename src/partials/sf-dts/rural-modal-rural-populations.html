Duty to Serve rural populations

Supporting Native American Tribes

326 Native American reservations

Key housing challenges:

  • Legal complexities of land titles make mortgage financing difficult
  • Limited access to financial and homebuying education
  • Substandard housing

Here’s what we’re doing in these rural populations

  • Increasing homebuyer access to education and resources for members of a Federally Recognized Indian Tribe in Indian areas. This is critical to developing more informed, mortgage-ready borrowers and is key to responsible, sustainable homeownership. We’re collaborating with South Dakota’s Homebuyer Education Resource Organization (HERO) to provide resources, housing counseling certifications and homebuyer education to Native American organizations supporting tribal areas in South Dakota. Freddie Mac will work jointly with HERO to train and certify homebuyer education counselors and expand these resources to other tribal areas.
  • Increasing technical expertise in Indian areas
  • Developing a robust renovation mortgage product to help rehabilitate aging houses.
