About Us

We are on a mission to expand affordable rental housing for all Americans – while building a better housing finance system.

As the leader in multifamily finance, we marry innovative solutions with expert risk management. Over 90% of apartments we fund are affordable for moderate- to low- income renters. And the vast majority of risk is transferred to private investors.

It’s all part of our continuous quest to champion a better housing finance system – one that's good for the industry, the investors and America's taxpayers.
