Partnership Profile

Native American Homebuyer Outreach with South Dakota’s Homebuyer Education Resource Organization (HERO)

Freddie Mac, in collaboration with the South Dakota Native Homeownership Coalition and Seven Sisters Community Development Group, LLC, has partnered with South Dakota’s Homeownership Education Resource Organization (HERO) to help put aspiring Native American homeowners on the path to homeownership. As a first step, Freddie Mac is working with HERO to train and certify homebuyer education counselors. The training and certification component is a critical area to homebuyer education since the lack of counselors’ training and certification was recognized as a barrier to the 16 Native housing organizations identified as potential HERO Partners. Once certified, these counselors will be able to provide homebuyer education and financial literacy to Native Americans in the South Dakotan Indian areas.
