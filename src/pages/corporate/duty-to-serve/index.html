Through Duty to Serve, we’re expanding our mission to tackle the nation’s most persistent housing challenges

Tackling the Affordability Crisis

Our nation is facing a housing affordability crisis. It affects around 40 million households today, and the problem is expected to continue to grow. We welcome the opportunity to lead the industry in developing innovative solutions that will benefit our country for generations to come.

Supporting Rural Housing

68% of U.S. counties are considered rural.

With our low down payment Home Possible mortgages, we're helping to break down barriers for qualified first-time homebuyers and low- to moderate-income borrowers.

We’re working to create meaningful rural housing opportunities for more households:

Improve options for financing renovations

Collaborate with USDA to support housing for low-income earners

Re-engage in the Low-Income Housing Tax Credit equity market

Supporting Manufactured Housing

12% of low-income households live in manufactured homes.

Our SmartMH program with Freddie Mac is bringing together leaders in the manufactured housing industry, lenders and housing counselors to foster better educated, mortgage-ready homebuyers.

Chris Nicely
CEO Next Step

We’re expanding financing for manufactured homes:

Buy loans on homes titled as real or personal property

Support communities with tenant protections

Support resident-owned communities

Supporting Affordable Housing Preservation

27% of renters today spend more than half their incomes on rent vs. 20% of them in 2000 and 12% in 1960.

We constantly innovate to find new and better ways to preserve affordable rental housing.

We’re focusing on preserving affordable housing to foster strong, stable communities:

Buy more loans on rental properties with federal subsidies to help keep rents low and improve living conditions

Promote energy-efficiency upgrades

Standardize residential market infrastructure and financing

Making a Positive Difference Together

We’re engaging with the industry, community organizations, all levels of government, and other dedicated organizations to take action.

Working together, we’re building a better future.
